Ai Pong Clash Privacy Policy

General

This Privacy Policy enters into force as of March 2025 (Effective Date).

Ai Pong Clash does not collect personal or game-related data. However, trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics and Crashlytics may automatically collect anonymized usage data for performance tracking and issue resolution.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via an in-game message or announcement 30 days in advance.

1. Data Collection

Ai Pong Clash does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Third-party services may collect anonymous usage data for analytics and debugging purposes.

Automatically Collected Data (by Third Parties)

  • Device information: IP address, device type, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Usage data: Gameplay statistics, levels completed, and crash reports.

2. Purpose of Data Usage

  1. To analyze game performance and improve features.
  2. To detect and resolve technical issues or bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

We may share anonymous usage data with trusted third-party services:

  • Analytics services: Google Analytics is used for performance tracking, with anonymized data.
  • Crash reporting: Crashlytics helps identify and fix technical issues.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose anonymous data if required by law.

4. Children's Privacy

Ai Pong Clash complies with COPPA and GDPR-K regulations. Since we do not collect personal data, no information is stored from children under 13. However, third-party services like Google Analytics may collect anonymized data beyond our control.

For more details, contact us at highapps2019@gmail.com.

If you have any questions, reach out to highapps2019@gmail.com.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Updates will be posted in-game.
