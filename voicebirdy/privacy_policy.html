Voice Birdy Privacy Policy

General

This Privacy Policy enters into force as of January 2025 (Effective Date).

At Voice Birdy, your privacy is our priority. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play Voice Birdy, a game designed for entertainment with unique voice control capabilities. Please read this policy carefully before using our game. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please refrain from using our services.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our game or legal requirements. If significant changes are made, we will notify you through an in-game message or announcement 30 days in advance. Continued use of the game constitutes your agreement to the updated terms.

1. Types of data and how we collect it

We collect minimal data necessary to provide and improve your gaming experience. Data is collected either directly or automatically as follows:

Direct

This includes information you voluntarily provide when playing Voice Birdy:

  • Your username or nickname (optional).
  • Game progress and high scores.

Automatic

We collect certain data automatically to enhance gameplay and troubleshoot issues. This may include:

  • Device information: IP address, device type, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Usage data: Gameplay statistics, levels completed, and crash reports.
  • Voice input data: For the voice control feature, we process voice commands locally on your device. No voice recordings are stored or transmitted to our servers.

2. Reasons we use your data

We collect and use your data for the following purposes:

  1. To provide a smooth and enjoyable gaming experience.
  2. To analyze game performance and improve features.
  3. To detect and resolve technical issues or bugs.
  4. To personalize your gameplay experience.

3. Third parties and your data

We may share your data with trusted third parties for specific purposes:

  • Analytics services: We use tools like Google Analytics to understand game performance and player behavior. Data shared with these tools is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Ad providers: If ads are displayed in the game, basic device information may be shared with ad networks to serve relevant ads. No personal data is shared for targeted advertising.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose your data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Your rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access and review the data we collect about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Restrict or object to certain data processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection (e.g., opt-out of analytics or ads).

To exercise your rights, please contact us at highapps2019@gmail.com.

5. Keeping your account secure

Protecting your data is our priority. While Voice Birdy does not require account creation, any data collected is stored securely with encryption. Please ensure your device is protected from unauthorized access.

6. Cookie Policy

Cookies are used to improve the game's functionality and performance. These small data files help track gameplay preferences and session data. You can disable cookies in your browser or device settings, but some features may not function properly as a result.

7. Contact us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our team at highapps2019@gmail.com.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy in the game. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for updates.
